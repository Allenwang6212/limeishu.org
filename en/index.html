Traffic Details

Taking HSR/TRA

1.Taking HSR/TRA to Banqiao Station.
2.Following the index and go to Banqiao Bus Station.
3.Take the bus (Taipei Passenger, 910) and get off at Anxi Ｅlementary School Station (Li Mei-shu Memorial Gallery).
4.After about 2 minutes walk to the Li Mei-Shu Memorial Gallery.

Ridding MRT

1.Take the MRT Blue Line (Bannan Line/Line 5).
2.You can get off at Xinpu Station, Banqiao Station, Yongning Station and Dingpu Station, Following the index and go to Bus Station.
3.Take the bus to Li Mei-Shu Memorial Gallery（Details for click tag "Bus"）.

By Bus

1.From Banqiao & Xinpu, you can take the both 910 and 940 bus to Anxi Ｅlementary School Station (Li Mei-shu Memorial Gallery) and get off, then walk about 2 minutes to arrive.
2.From Tucheng & Ding-Pu, you can take the both 705 and 706 bus to Jiao-Xi li. Station is about a 10-minute walk away; or take 916 bus to Sanxia Street, then walk about 10 minutes to arrive.
3.From Taipei/Dunhua S. Rd./Taipei City Hall, you can take the 939 bus to Taipei University Sanxia Campus Station, then walk about 20 minutes to arrive.
4.From Shulin/Xin-Zhuang, you can take the both 702 and 802 bus to Sanxia Street Station, then walk about 10 minutes to arrive.
5.From Zhonghe/Yonghe , you can take the 908 bus to Sanxia Ｅlementary School Station and get off, then walk about 15 minutes to arrive.

Driving Car

1.Driving alone the Formosa Freeway 3rd and exit from the Sanxia gateway.
2.Trun to county road nb. 110 (Fu-Xing Rd.) and go straght.
3.Trun right at Zhunghua Rd. (will saw a flyover), go straght and trun right at the intersection in front of Anxi Ｅlementary School.
4.Park at Changfu Park.（Changfu Park is located at the right side of Guang-Ming Rd. Click here for map.）
Other Parking Place.
